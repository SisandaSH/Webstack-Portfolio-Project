About Us

Your Dining Decision Helper

Created by Sisanda Shwala and Sthabile Shwala, Restaurant Chooser is a user-friendly app designed to simplify your dining decisions. We understand that choosing where to eat can sometimes be overwhelming, so we've created this tool to make the process quick, easy, and fun.

Our app presents you with a curated selection of popular restaurant chains, each offering unique cuisines and dining experiences. With just a click, you can explore options ranging from Roccomamas' gourmet burgers to Panarottis' delicious pizzas and pasta.

Whether you're in the mood for the bold flavors of Hungry Lion, the cozy atmosphere of Mugg & Bean, the fresh catch at Fishaways, or the seafood delights at Ocean Basket, Restaurant Chooser has you covered.

Simply browse through the restaurant options, click "Order Now" on your choice, and let your taste buds guide you to your next great meal. Restaurant Chooser - making your "What's for dinner?" dilemma a thing of the past!
